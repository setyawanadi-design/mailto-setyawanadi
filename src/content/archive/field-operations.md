## Role Summary

**Field Operations Coordinator** — coordinating multi-site retail deployments and managing on-the-ground teams for POSM installations and promotional setups.

## Key Responsibilities

- Coordinated deployment schedules for teams across **15+ retail locations**
- Managed inventory logistics including **receiving, cataloging, and distributing** promotional materials
- Maintained daily communication with site managers, vendors, and headquarters
- Generated weekly operational reports tracking **completion rates, issues, and resource utilization**
- Troubleshot on-site problems including shipping delays, material shortages, and staffing issues

## Achievements

- Improved deployment completion rate from 78% to **94%** through better scheduling
- Reduced material waste by **30%** through improved inventory tracking
- Developed a spreadsheet-based reporting system that cut report generation time by **50%**

## Lessons Learned

This role taught me that operations success comes down to three things: **preparation, communication, and adaptability**. No amount of planning can account for every variable on the ground, but thorough preparation gives you the foundation to handle surprises.
