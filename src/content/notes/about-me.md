Hi!

I'm **Adi Setyawan**, an operations professional based in Southeast Asia with a passion for field team management, logistics coordination, and making systems work better.

## What I do

I specialize in **full-cycle field operations** — from planning and scheduling to on-the-ground execution. My experience includes:

- **Team deployment & coordination** across multiple sites
- **Inventory management** and logistics planning
- **POSM (Point of Sale Materials) deployment** for retail and marketing campaigns
- **Operational reporting** and performance tracking
- **Cross-functional communication** between stakeholders, vendors, and field teams

## My approach

I believe in three things:

1. **Clarity over complexity** — Simple systems that everyone understands beat sophisticated ones that nobody uses
2. **Hands-on leadership** — The best managers are the ones who know what the work actually looks like on the ground
3. **Continuous improvement** — There's always a better way to do things, and I'm always looking for it

## Beyond work

When I'm not managing operations, you'll find me tinkering with web technologies (like this site!), exploring new tools, and learning about design systems. I'm also a Mozilla Thunderbird enthusiast — which is probably obvious from the look of this site.
